Our Practice Areas

Firm Overview

Dilles Law Group, PC specializes in the representation of injured workers before the Workers' Compensation Appeals Board. Workers' compensation law is one of the most complicated areas of California law. Our firm has a reputation for quality trial skills and in-depth knowledge of California workers' compensation laws.

Workers' Compensation

Our attorneys have experience and skill helping injured workers obtain the workers' compensation benefits that are legally owed to them.
Public Safety Representation

Our attorneys are experts in representing public safety officers injured on the job and in the presumptions and benefits available to these officers.
Subsequent Injuries Benefits Trust Fund

Our attorneys have extensive knowledge of the Subsequent Injuries Benefits Trust Fund (SIBTF) and how to qualify for these further monetary benefits.

Why Choose Us?

We are dedicated to representing public safety professionals and all workers injured on the job, providing a legal team that fights for your rights with honesty, transparency, and integrity.
